THE WEDDING

You may have noticed this on your invite, and it's important...

Don't dress for the wedding, dress for the party!

We want all our guests to come and celebrate with us and enjoy their day, so we want you to feel comfortable and like you!

The order of the day will go something like this:

  • 12:00 - Guests start arriving
  • 12:30 - Ceremony
  • 13:00 - Reception drinks & photos
  • 15:00 - Vintage Afternoon Tea
  • 17:30 - Speeches
  • 19:00 - Evening guests start arriving
  • 19:30 - Cutting the cake
  • 21:00 - Evening food
  • 00:00 - Home time for those not staying at Caer Llan

Drinks

There is a bar (obviously) which has pretty reasonable prices. The bar will open after the ceremony. There will also be some wine on the table. You're not allowed to bring any alcohol to Caer Llan with you. There is free tea & coffee available for all guests, all day.

Vintage Afternoon Tea Menu

The Vintage Afternoon Tea (which looks amazing!) consists of:

  • Sandwiches (on a mix of granary & white bread)
    • Cream cheese & cucumber
    • Smoked salmon with lemon creme fraiche
    • Free-range egg & cress
    • Ham & mustard
  • Savouries (a selection of homemade savoury party bites)
    • Pork pies
    • Scotch eggs
    • Sausage & veggie rolls
    • A selection of mini quiche
  • Homemade cakes & dainties (a selection to satisfy the sweet tooth)
    • Victoria sponge cake
    • Coffee & walnut cake
    • Scones with strawberry jam & clotted cream
    • Chocolate dipped strawberries
  • Freshly brewed tea

Evening Food Menu

Bacon baps (veggie sausage baps for the vegetarian guests) and wedges will be served at 9pm.
