Accommodation

For more information about staying at Caer Llan (price, check in and out time, what's included etc), see the accommodation page.

As mentioned on the accommodation page, if you are staying with us at Caer Llan we ask that you settle the balance for your room before the start of November. Payment details will be sent out when we confirm your room. We will aim to confirm rooms ASAP so if we end up running out of rooms, you will have plenty of time to book elsewhere.
