Katy & Sarah's Wedding

18th November 2017 @ Caer Llan

Who could have known when we met all those years ago at University that 13 years later, we'd be getting married!

We survived copious amounts of alcohol, exams, first jobs, crap jobs, long distances (mostly), moving across the country, becoming mothers to a slightly derranged scruffy dog and buying our first house. On top of all of that, Sarah even managed to convert Katy to a fully-fledged, literally card carrying, Eurovision fan.

We really hope you can join us to celebrate all of that and more!

This site should hopefully be your go-to place for any information about our big day. You can find information about things like the venue, the day and the local accommodation and we'll keep updating with any more useful information.

Most importantly though, this site will help you RSVP which we hope you'll do for us really soon.
