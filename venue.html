VENUE

To celebrate our day we chose the beautiful Caer Llan. It's an old country house with amazing views over the Welsh mountains that's now run by the family that lives there as an events venue. For more information on the venue, visit their website.

Getting there

If you're driving to the venue, the postcode for your sat nav is NP25 4JS. Please note when travelling to Caer Llan using sat nav, they have a shared postcode with a neighbouring property. Some sat navs will give directions to the other property, 200 yards away from Caer Llan.

Parking

There is limited parking available at Caer Llan (maximum 40 spaces). If you are staying at Caer Llan (find more on this on the accommodation page), you should park in the bottom car park. Guests driving to us for the day can park in the top car park.
